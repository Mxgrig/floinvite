## Visual Elements Standards

- Overlay text: "Construction Visitor Management Made Easy"

### Content Images
- Screenshots of clean, professional interface
- Before/after comparisons (clipboard vs tablet)
- Infographics showing time savings

### CTA Buttons
- Orange/red for construction industry
- Text: "Get Free Checklist" / "Start Trial" / "View Demo"
- Prominent placement above fold

## Personalization Variables

- {First Name} - From contact data
- {Company} - Company name
- {Job Title} - If available (Project Manager, Site Supervisor, etc.)
- {Location} - City/state for local relevance
- {Pain Point} - Dynamic based on email history

## A/B Testing Framework

### Subject Line Tests
1. Problem vs Solution: "Time Tracking Problems" vs "Time Tracking Solutions"
2. Urgency: With vs without "Last Chance"
3. Personalization: Generic vs "John - Construction Solutions"

### Content Tests
1. Length: Short (150 words) vs Detailed (300 words)
2. Images: With photos vs Text-only
3. CTA Position: Top vs Bottom vs Middle

### Send Time Tests
1. Tuesday 9AM vs Thursday 10AM
2. Mid-week vs End-of-week

These templates address construction-specific pain points, provide immediate value, and follow proven email marketing best practices for the industry.
